Registration and Activation
Register for an account using a valid email address. An activation email will be sent to this address.
Logging In
Log in with your registered username and password.
User Interaction
Upon initial login, you will be recognized as a voter in our interface. If you wish to create an election, kindly reach out to us via our provided email or phone number. Alternatively, you can use the "CONTACT US" form on our homepage to get in touch.
Admin Access

Once logged in as an admin:

  1. i. You can create an election. Please note that only one election can be created at a time. After an election is completed, you can create another by deleting the previous one.
  2. ii. You have the capability to create desired positions and candidates for the election, and you can manage them as needed. .
  3. iii. You have access to the voters list.
  4. v. From the Admin dashboard, you can access detailed information about the currently running election. In the event that any issues arise, you can reset the election by navigating to the "MORE INFO" section within the votes interface.
  5. vi. After the election concludes, you can generate result printouts with the winners in specific positions.
  6. vii. It is mandatory to delete the election after it concludes. This ensures that voters can participate in other elections.
  7. viii. If an admin wishes to vote, they can select the election they desire to vote in.
Voter Participation

When logged in as a voter:

  1. i. Select the election in which you wish to vote or choose your desired candidates.
  2. ii. Only currently running elections will be available for selection.
  3. iii. After casting your vote, you can preview your selected candidates and then submit your ballot.
  4. v. In the voter dashboard, you can access details about the election you have participated in.

These steps outline the process of registration, administration, and participation in our election system, ensuring a seamless and professional experience for all users.
